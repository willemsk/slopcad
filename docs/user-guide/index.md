# Getting Started with SlopCAD

Welcome to the **SlopCAD** User Guide! This guide will walk you through the basics of starting a project, using the editor interface, drawing your first plan, and exporting your design.

---

## 🏗️ Interface Overview

SlopCAD is inspired by traditional CAD desktop applications like AutoCAD. The screen is divided into several main areas:

1.  **Main Canvas**: The large central area where your 2D design is drawn.
2.  **Left Toolbar**: Contains shortcuts to all drawing tools (Wall, Door, Window, Line, Rect, etc.) and editing tools (Select, Delete).
3.  **Top Menu Bar / Properties**: Shows general settings, undo/redo, saving/loading, and detailed properties of any selected entity.
4.  **Status Bar**: Located at the bottom right, it contains toggles for Grid snapping, Object snapping, UCS representation, and scale.
5.  **Command Prompt**: Located at the bottom left, showing logs and history.

---

## 🚶 Step-by-Step: Your First Floor Plan

Follow these steps to create a simple room:

### Step 1: Draw a Wall
1. Select the **Wall Tool** from the left toolbar (or press `W` if keyboard shortcuts are configured).
2. Click on the canvas to place the starting point of the wall.
3. Move your mouse to define the direction and length. Click again to place the wall endpoint.
4. Right-click or press `Escape` to stop drawing.

### Step 2: Add a Door
1. Click the **Door Tool** from the left toolbar.
2. Hover over the wall you drew. The door will snap to align with the wall.
3. Click to place the door along the wall.

### Step 3: Add a Window
1. Select the **Window Tool** from the left toolbar.
2. Hover over a wall and click to insert the window.

### Step 4: Export to SVG
1. Click **File -> Export** or the Export button in the menu bar.
2. Download your plan as a vector SVG file, ready for viewing or printing!
